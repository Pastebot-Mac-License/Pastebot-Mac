## How to Install

1. Download the latest version of Pastebot for macOS.  
2. Open the DMG installer and move Pastebot.app to the Applications folder.  
3. Launch the app and allow it through macOS Gatekeeper if required.  

---

## Overview

<a href="#">Pastebot for macOS</a> is a clipboard manager that makes it easy to store and reuse your clipboard items. It records everything you copy, from text and links to images and files, allowing you to paste them again at any time. With Pastebot, you never have to worry about losing important data that you’ve copied, making it a must-have tool for anyone who works with multiple items at once.

The app’s powerful search feature lets you find anything you’ve copied in seconds. You can also categorize your clipboard items with labels, making it easier to organize your past copies. With customizable shortcuts and the ability to sync across devices, Pastebot enhances your productivity by making your clipboard more powerful and organized.

On macOS, Pastebot integrates seamlessly with the system, offering a smooth and efficient way to handle your clipboard history. It’s a time-saving tool that can help you improve your workflow, whether you're working on documents, programming, or managing complex tasks that require lots of copying and pasting.

---

## Major Features
- Manage clipboard history and organize clippings into categories  
- Retrieve copied text, images, and links with ease  
- Access clipboard history directly from the menu bar  
- Saves everything copied for easy retrieval later  
- Streamlines the copy-paste process across apps and tools  
- Enhances productivity by reducing the need to copy repeatedly
